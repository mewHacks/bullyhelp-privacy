## 2. Information We Collect  
BullyHelp collects only minimal personal information required for safety and reporting purposes:  

- **Student profile data:** name, school, class, and parent/guardian email.  
- **Location data:** collected only during SOS activation or incident reporting.  
- **Audio recording (optional):** short 15-second clip captured during SOS trigger (deleted if cancelled).  
- **Anonymous reports:** text and optional image/video when students report bullying incidents.  

No other personal information is requested or collected.

Your data is used **solely** for the following purposes:  
- To send emergency alerts and incident reports to your registered guardian(s).  
- To provide accurate location details during SOS events.  
- To allow students to review past SOS history locally.  
- To analyze and detect bullying language for educational purposes only (via ML Kit).
