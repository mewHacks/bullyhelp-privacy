# BullyHelp Privacy Statement  

BullyHelp respects your privacy.  
The app collects limited personal information — your name, school, parent email, and location — **only** to support emergency SOS and reporting functions.  

All data is stored locally on your device and sent securely (via HTTPS) only to your approved guardian.  
No personal data is shared, sold, or used for advertising.  

You can view or delete your data at any time.  
For full details, please read our complete [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/mewHacks/bullyhelp-privacy/blob/main/privacy-policy.md).
